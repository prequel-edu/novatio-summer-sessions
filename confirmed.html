HOW NOVATIO WORKS

Two hours of school. A full afternoon of life.

Novatio students master their academics in two focused, AI-personalized hours each morning — then spend their afternoons on real-world skills. Done by 2pm. No homework.

AI-driven learning personalized to your child's exact level — not their grade, their actual skill. Two focused hours, then they're done.

Arizona and Texas have education savings account programs that cover Novatio in full. If you're outside those states, financial aid is available.
