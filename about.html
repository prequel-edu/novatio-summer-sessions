HOW NOVATIO WORKS

Two hours of school. A full afternoon of life.

Novatio is built around a simple idea: students don't need six hours of seat time to master academics. Two focused, personalized hours is enough — and that frees up the rest of the day for something more.

🌅
8:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Morning Academic Block

Students log in anytime during the 8–10am window for a 2-hour focused learning session. AI-driven, personalized to their exact level — not their grade, their actual skill.

  • 30-min Morning Launch with teacher (games, puzzles, mindfulness)
  • Personalized academic work across 5 subjects
  • 25-min work / 5-min break rhythm — proven for deep focus
  • 1:1 academic coach + AI tutor always available
  • 80% daily mastery standard — no moving on until it sticks
🎨
12:00 PM – 2:00 PM

Novatio Clubs (Afternoons)

Students choose 4 clubs per session. Each club is built around a real-world life skill — not "busy work." Taught by teachers who love what they're doing.

  • Entrepreneurship, coding, financial literacy, public speaking
  • Art, robotics, teamwork, goal-setting
  • Project-based — students leave with real work to show
  • Harvard Business School simulation (students outperform MBAs)
  • Friends from across the country who share their interests

TUITION

Most families pay nothing.

Arizona and Texas have education savings account programs that cover Novatio in full. If you're outside those states, financial aid is available.

Arizona — ESA
$0

The Arizona Empowerment Scholarship Account (ESA) covers $7,000/year — enough to fully fund Novatio. Every AZ K–12 student qualifies. No income limit.

Texas — TEFA
$0

Texas Education Freedom Accounts (TEFA) cover Novatio for eligible TX families. Check your eligibility with Karissa on your call.

Out of State
$10K

$10,000/year for families outside AZ and TX. Financial aid is available — book a call with Karissa to discuss your options.
