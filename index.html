WHAT IS NOVATIO

A school built around your child,
not the other way around.

Novatio is a private, virtual, AI-driven school for grades 4–8. Students master their academics in just two focused hours each morning, then spend their afternoons on real-world skills — coding, entrepreneurship, financial literacy, public speaking, and more.

Students grow 4.6× faster than in traditional school. School's done by 2pm. No homework.
