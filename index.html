QUESTIONS

Good to know before you sign up.

Is this live or recorded?
Live. Your child joins an interactive Zoom session with a teacher and other students. They participate, ask questions, and build in real time — it's not a video to watch.
What does my child need to join?
A laptop or desktop computer — not a phone or tablet (screen size matters for these activities). They'll also need a Gmail account, either their own or a parent's. We'll have options ready on the day if they need help getting set up.
Is this really free?
Yes. No credit card, no application, no hidden fees. Just pick a session, sign up, and show up.
We're a homeschool family — is this for us?
Absolutely. These sessions were built with homeschool families in mind. Your child will be alongside other virtual and homeschooled students who understand what it's actually like to learn outside a traditional classroom.
Does my child need to prepare anything?
Nothing. The teacher walks through everything from the start. Just join on time and be ready to participate. That's it.
Can my child sign up for more than one session?
Yes — and we recommend it. Each session is a completely different activity, so there's no overlap. Sign up for all three in the same week of June or spread them across the month.
What grades are these sessions for?
Students in grades 2–8 (for the 2026–2027 school year). The sessions are designed to be accessible and engaging across that full range.
